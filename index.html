Your AI. Your way. Pick a vibe, pick a personality, start talking. Each companion remembers everything about you — from your K-pop bias list to your wedding budget to your grief over losing a pet. Free for 48 hours, no signup, no credit card.

Why My Very Own is different from ChatGPT and Claude

Generic AI assistants forget everything between conversations. My Very Own personas are pre-tuned for a single purpose with persistent memory powered by Cortex. The relationship actually deepens over time as the AI learns about you.

How it works

  1. Pick a persona from the homepage showcase — 20 options cycling through different life moments and interests.
  2. Tell the AI your name. No email, no account, no credit card required.
  3. Chat for 48 hours free. The AI remembers everything from message one.
  4. When you're ready, sign up with a magic link email or Google account. Your memory migrates to your authenticated account.
  5. Subscribe to keep the relationship permanent ($4.99 to $19.99 per month depending on the persona).

Technology stack

My Very Own runs on Gemini 2.0 Flash — Google's fast, cost-efficient LLM — combined with Cortex, a self-developing cognitive architecture that provides persistent memory, semantic search, and pattern detection. The platform is built with React + Vite on the frontend (deployed to Vercel) and Node.js + TypeScript on the backend (deployed to Railway), with Stripe handling subscriptions.

Built by Number One Son Software Development

My Very Own is built by Number One Son Software Development, a solopreneur studio working toward a $1 billion valuation by running 20 niche AI companion products on a shared codebase. The same engine powers every persona — only the skin, voice, and memory namespace change per product.

Source code is open and available on GitHub: myveryown (landing), myveryown-api (backend), myveryown-web (React app), and cortex (memory engine).
